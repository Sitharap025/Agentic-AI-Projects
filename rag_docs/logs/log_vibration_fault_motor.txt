Incident Date: 2023-09-21
Machine ID: M004

Symptom:
- Continuous vibration alert triggered
- Audible rattling from motor housing
- Slight misalignment in belt drive

Diagnosis:
Motor misalignment due to loose mounting bolts caused sustained vibrations. Prolonged exposure could have damaged the bearings.

Actions Taken:
1. Realigned motor shaft and retightened mounts
2. Replaced worn bearings
3. Vibration dampening pads installed
4. Alert threshold adjusted in monitoring system

Resolution Time: 3 hours
Failure Severity: High
