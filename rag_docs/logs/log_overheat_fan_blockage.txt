Incident Date: 2023-11-02
Machine ID: M002

Symptom:
- Rapid temperature increase
- High RPM readings (3400+)
- Unusual mechanical noise

Diagnosis:
Inspection revealed the air intake filter was clogged with particulate matter, reducing airflow to the motor. The RPM limit was also exceeded during peak load, exacerbating heat accumulation.

Actions Taken:
1. Air filter replaced and vent cleaned
2. RPM governor reset to max 3000
3. Thermal sensors recalibrated
4. Preventive maintenance schedule updated

Resolution Time: 1.5 hours
Failure Severity: Medium
