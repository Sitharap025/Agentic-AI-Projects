Standard Operating Procedure: Oil System Maintenance
Version: 1.3 | Last Updated: 2023-12-18

Objective:
Ensure oil systems remain contaminant-free and operate under optimal pressure conditions.

Weekly Routine:
- Inspect oil levels and check for discoloration
- Monitor pressure gauges for irregular spikes
- Use dipstick test to check for metal residue
- Verify seals are intact and dry

Monthly Tasks:
- Conduct full oil sample analysis
- Check pump and valve wear
- Review pressure trends in monitoring logs

Critical Replacement:
- Replace oil every 200 days OR
- Immediately if viscosity drops by 15% OR
- After exposure to abnormal temperature or contamination

Approved Oil Types:
- Grade 68 (standard)
- Grade 100 (high-load conditions)
