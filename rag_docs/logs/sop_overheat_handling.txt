Standard Operating Procedure: Overheat Event Handling
Version: 2.0 | Last Updated: 2024-03-12

Objective:
Guide operators through steps to manage overheating equipment conditions safely and efficiently.

Steps:
1. Immediately reduce machine load by 30–50%
2. Check air vents and cooling fans for blockage
3. Initiate active cooling system if available
4. Log temperature and RPM readings
5. Notify the maintenance engineer if temp exceeds 80°C
6. Resume normal operations only after full inspection and written clearance

Preventive Tips:
- Clean air filters weekly
- Do not exceed recommended RPM
- Replace cooling fluids every 90 days
